Omelette Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a simple delicious Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Use cooking spray on medium sized pan and set heat to medium/medium low
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk vigorously
  3. Pour egges into pan and wait for to begin to solidify
  4. Down middle of omelette add cheese and ham and season with salt and pepper
  5. Once eggs are sufficiantly cooked flip one side of egg over cheese and then flip left side of egg over cheese
  6. Remove omelette from pan and enjoy!
