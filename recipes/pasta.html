Pasta

Description

This is a recipe for pasta im not trying to write a whole essay on this but a few sentence should do

Now this will do the trick im not trying to share my secret sauce with anybody

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • water
  • salt
  • secret sauce
  • chives

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add salt to water
  3. Add Pasta
  4. Let pasta boil for 8 minutes
  5. Take pot off heat and drain water
  6. Add secret sauce and chives
  7. Slurp and Enjoy
