Hamburger Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a simple delicious Hamburger

Ingredients

  • One pound of ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degress
  2. Form hamburger into patties and seeason with salt and pepper
  3. Put patties in oven
  4. Let patties cook for 25 minutes
  5. Pull patties from oven and add cheese and onions enjoy
